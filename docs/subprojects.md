# Polis Subprojects

## Agora

Role inside Polis:

- visible demand layer
- builder response and delivery layer
- customer, builder, and ops surfaces

Core contribution:

Agora makes Polis concrete by showing where AI economy coordination becomes an
actual operational system.

## Noesis

Role inside Polis:

- human-machine symbiosis research
- institutional adaptation
- social and economic observability

Core contribution:

Noesis gives Polis a serious theory layer about how intelligence systems change
institutions, labor, legitimacy, and coordination.

## Peras

Role inside Polis:

- frontier signal ingestion
- ranking and thesis updates
- future-value judgment

Core contribution:

Peras keeps Polis forward-looking by turning frontier movement into usable
judgment instead of leaving the umbrella as a static market-and-theory bundle.

## Chorus

Role inside Polis:

- human + agent deliberation rooms
- visible interaction graphs
- minority reports and synthesis artifacts
- public reasoning surfaces

Core contribution:

Chorus gives Polis a live social-reasoning layer where humans and independent
agents can debate, route evidence, and converge into inspectable conclusions.
