# Overview

Peras is the portfolio project for AI frontier intelligence:

an information platform that watches frontier academic research, commercial
movement, industrial adoption, and historical patterns, then uses AI
reasoning to continuously update which directions have the highest future
value.

Peras now sits inside `Polis`, where it acts as the judgment engine paired with
Agora's market surface and Noesis's institutional observatory.

## Product Thesis

Most people trying to spot valuable AI directions are stuck in one of three bad
modes:

- chasing hype without source discipline
- reading only academia and missing product reality
- reading only commercial news and missing frontier capability shifts

Peras should solve that by combining:

- live frontier sources
- historical-pattern memory
- AI synthesis and ranking
- explicit thesis updates over time

## Main Users

- founders choosing what to build
- investors deciding what is real versus theatrical
- researchers and operators mapping academic progress to product value

## First Outputs

- ranked opportunity maps
- recurring frontier updates
- thesis cards with evidence
- explanation of why a direction is rising or falling
