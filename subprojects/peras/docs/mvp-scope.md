# MVP Scope

## In

- source architecture for academic + commercial + industry signals
- first opportunity-thesis framework
- periodic AI-generated ranking updates
- historical-pattern integration as a judgment aid
- evidence-backed opportunity cards for founders and investors

## Out

- pretending the platform can predict the future with certainty
- ingesting low-quality social-noise sources as if they were real evidence
- trading advice or financial guarantees
- overclaiming that all important future value is machine-inferable
