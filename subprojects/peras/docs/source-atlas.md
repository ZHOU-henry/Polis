## Source Rule

Peras should favor:

- official APIs
- top conferences and journals
- official research labs and institutes
- market sources with strong provenance

Peras should distrust:

- hype feeds
- screenshots as evidence
- influencer summaries
- low-quality scraping targets that cannot sustain a real product
