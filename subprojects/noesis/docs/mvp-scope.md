# MVP Scope

## In

- human-machine symbiosis framing
- social-evolution framing for the AGI era
- governance and institutional redesign questions
- artificial-consciousness and machine-subjectivity questions as a formal subproject
- computation-boundary and machine-limit questions as a formal subproject
- AI-economy six-factor systems questions as a formal subproject
- evidence-disciplined scenario work
- explicit separation between evidence, inference, and speculation

## Out

- claiming AGI arrival timelines as settled fact
- mystical or ungrounded consciousness rhetoric
- pretending the final political or civilizational model is already known
- vague futurism without clear research output structure
