## Source Discipline

Noesis should prioritize:

- official institutions
- top journals and conferences
- data infrastructures
- labs with visible research programs

Noesis should deprioritize:

- hype threads
- weak blog summaries
- unsourced speculation
- low-rigor popular futurism
