# Roadmap

## Phase 1

Define the argument structure.

Deliverables:

- project charter
- problem map
- research lanes
- first scenario clusters
- explicit subproject map:
  - artificial consciousness
  - computation boundaries
  - AI economy six-factor substrate
  - communicative rationality and AI-era coordination

## Phase 2

Build a serious AGI symbiosis research program.

Deliverables:

- human-machine role taxonomy
- institutional redesign briefs
- labor, education, and governance scenario packs
- clear open-question registry
- subproject-A synthesis:
  machine subjectivity, consciousness, and neuroscience-facing questions
- subproject-B synthesis:
  machine limits, computational boundaries, complexity, and practical AI structure
- subproject-C synthesis:
  data, compute, storage, energy, algorithms, and talent as the economic substrate of AGI society
  plus the first six-factor observatory specification for quantification and visualization
- subproject-D synthesis:
  communicative rationality, legitimacy, post-price coordination, and
  machine-assisted economic design

## Phase 3

Turn Noesis into a durable observatory.

Deliverables:

- recurring synthesis reports
- tracked indicators
- comparative country / institution cases
- public-facing research memos and policy framing
- cross-subproject integration memos that connect:
  subjectivity, machine limits, economic substrate, communicative
  coordination, and AGI social design
