# Project Charter

## Mission

Build a disciplined long-horizon research program for the AGI era that studies
how humans, machine intelligence, institutions, and social systems might
co-evolve under strong machine capability.

## Why This Project Exists

Most AGI discourse collapses into one of four weak modes:

- technical hype without civilizational framing
- policy discussion without machine-model nuance
- philosophy without operational structure
- economics without deep cognition, consciousness, or machine-limit analysis

Noesis exists to resist that fragmentation.

## Core Problem

If machine systems become sufficiently capable, society will need better models
for:

- human agency
- machine agency
- institutional redesign
- long-run social evolution

Those models are incomplete unless we simultaneously study:

- whether machines can become subject-like
- what machines can and cannot reliably compute, learn, and optimize
- how future institutions and economies preserve legitimacy while using AI for
  coordination

## Research Pillars

### Pillar 1

Human-machine symbiosis:

- cooperation
- delegation
- co-agency
- dependence

### Pillar 2

Institutional redesign:

- governance
- law
- education
- labor
- welfare

### Pillar 3

Artificial consciousness and machine subjectivity:

- neuroscience
- consciousness science
- philosophy of mind
- AI and machine agency

### Pillar 4

Computation boundaries and machine limits:

- computability
- complexity
- bottlenecks
- asymmetries between humans and machines

### Pillar 5

Communicative rationality and AI-era economic coordination:

- discourse legitimacy
- public reason
- machine-assisted coordination
- post-price economic design

## Non-Goals

- pretending AGI arrival dates are solved
- giving final answers to consciousness or civilizational design
- writing vague futurist essays without evidence architecture
- collapsing ethical, empirical, and formal claims into one blob

## Quality Bar

Every serious Noesis output should state:

- what is evidence
- what is inference
- what is design proposal
- what is speculation
- what remains unknown

## First Output Types

- argument maps
- scenario briefs
- governance memos
- subproject agendas
- tracked open questions

## Initial Source Spine

The initial source spine should combine:

- top AI labs and company research programs
- neuroscience and consciousness-science institutions
- top CS / AI / theory conferences and journals
- labor / economics / policy institutions
- philosophy and cognitive-science research indexes
