# Third-Party Code Policy

## Rules

1. Do not copy code, figures, datasets, or papers without clear license terms.
2. Track source URL, license, and local modifications for every imported component.
3. Preserve all required attribution and notices.
4. Treat neuroscience data, benchmark outputs, and diagrams as provenance-sensitive artifacts.
5. Prefer summaries, adapters, and references over uncontrolled ingestion.

Open-source intent and philosophical ambition do not remove license obligations.
