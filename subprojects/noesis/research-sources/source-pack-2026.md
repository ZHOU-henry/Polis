## 4. What Changed Since The First Noesis Pass

The main upgrade in 2026 is that Noesis now has a better route to empirical
political economy:

- AI labor and macro framing are becoming more visible
- governance / deliberation software is more mature than generic AI policy talk
- the six-factor substrate can now plausibly become an observatory instead of a
  metaphor
